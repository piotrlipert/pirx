Printing Process

How does it work?

3D printing is achieved using an additive proces, where successive layers of plastic are laid down in different shapes.

See it for yourself:

Project

A 3D model is built using CAD software. Exported to popular STL format it is preprocessed and transfered to Pirx. Voila! Ready to print!

Material

Filament of heated thermoplastic polymer is squeezed out like toothpaste from a tube. The model is produced by extruding small beads of material to form layers.

Print

The desired object is printed layer after layer until it is complete. Ta-da! You have just produced something using your own personal factory. Enjoy!
